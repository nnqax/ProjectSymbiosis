Our Vision

Project Symbiosis's vision is that no human should suffer in silence and no human should be left behind without expressing their needs and having their voices heard. We aim to create a world where every person, regardless of their ability to communicate, can receive the care and attention they deserve. and that is what we are working towards with our innovative pain expression decoder.



The problem

Millions of people worldwide suffer from chronic pain, yet many of them struggle to communicate their pain effectively. This can lead to misdiagnosis, inadequate treatment, and a diminished quality of life. For individuals with conditions like fibromyalgia, arthritis, or neuropathic pain, the inability to express the intensity and nature of their pain can be particularly frustrating and isolating.

Our project addresses this critical gap by providing a non-invasive, objective solution for pain assessment.It's a multimodal AI system combining hyperspectral imaging (to detect subtle facial flushing and micro-expressions), vocal analysis (for sub-audible distress sounds), and biometric data (heart rate variability, galvanic skin response). It's trained specifically on patients who cannot communicate: neonates in NICUs, advanced dementia patients, paralyzed individuals, and patients under anesthesia.

This is a "voice for the voiceless." It doesn't replace nurses or doctors. It translates the patient's physiological state into a real-time, interpretable dashboard: "90% probability of moderate pain. Source likely abdominal." The care team can now treat pain that was previously invisible, humanizing care for the most vulnerable.And by that we produce a universal translator for suffering in those who cannot speak, transforming palliative care, post-surgical recovery, and end-of-life comfort.
