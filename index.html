Research & Results
99%

Cascella et al. proved that dual convolutional neural network models can reach up to 99% accuracy on pretrained datasets[cite: 7]. We also found that accuracy ranges from 80.9% to 89.59% for general AI pain detection[cite: 7].

Lessons Learned

  • FACS Method: We use the Facial Action Coding System to map muscle movements to pain[cite: 7].
  • Data Bias: We learned that bias can arise if data inputs are inaccurate, which is vital for our web system[cite: 8].
  • Multimodal Future: Combining facial data with heart rate leads to even better results[cite: 8].
