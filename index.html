Proposed Solution
We propose the development of a Pain Expression Decoder, an AI-powered system designed to interpret non-verbal cues of pain, particularly in patients who struggle to communicate verbally, such as children and elderly patients with dementia[cite: 23]. The decoder relies on facial expressions and body language to provide healthcare providers with real-time, interpretable assessments[cite: 25, 26].
